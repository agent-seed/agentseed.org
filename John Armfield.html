John Armfield

an american slave trader

Born in 1797, John Armfield was the co-founder of the largest slave trading company in the US, he also founded several other sucessful buisness. He took up slave trading sometime in the 1820s. In 1828 he and his uncle founded their infamous slave trading comapany. He and his uncle joked about raping then selling slaves, and he is know to have at lease on child with one of the slaves he sold. He made over a half a million dollars (not adjusted), transporting around 1,200 slaves a year.
